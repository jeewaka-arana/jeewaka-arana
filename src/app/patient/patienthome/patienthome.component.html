Why choose Jeewaka Arana?

Jeewaka Arana is Sri Lanka's online ayurvedic website, transforming the way Sri Lankans access and use healthcare services. We are driven to advance the accessibility of healthcare for all Sri Lankans by improving access to information and technology, and ultimately improving the experience of healthcare services. Jeewaka Arana makes finding and booking health appointments online quick and easy. Free for patients, our website allows you to navigate Sri Lanka's ayurvedic treatment system while providing practitioners with technology to improve the patient healthcare journey.
