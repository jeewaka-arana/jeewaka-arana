About us

Jeewaka Arana is the Sri Lanka's online ayurvedic website, transforming the way all can access and use healthcare services. We are driven to advance the accessibility of healthcare for all Sri Lankans and non-Sri lankans as well by improving access to information and technology, and ultimately improving the experience of healthcare services. Jeewaka Arana makes finding and booking health appointments online quick and easy. Free for patients, our website allows you to navigate Sri Lanka's ayurvedic/traditional treatment system while providing practitioners with technology to improve the patient healthcare journey.

Our Vision

Health for all through Ayurvedic and Indigenous Medical system

Our Mission

Formulation of policies required for the conservation of the people's health and maintaining good health conditions through the extension of Ayurveda System Island wide, maintain services for prevention of diseases, health care rehabilitation and development on an exemplary level, identify and implement strategies necessary for the propagation of Traditional Medical System globally
